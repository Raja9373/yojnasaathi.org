About Us - YojnaSaathi.org

YojnaSaathi.org was started in 2024 when my neighbor aunty had to pay Rs 1500 to get a ration card made. She did not know that ration cards are made for free at nfsa.gov.in. Then I understood that 1200+ government schemes are running in the country but 80% people do not even know about them.

I am Alok Mohan Sharma from Delhi, and my brother Maddy. We both thought of making a website where all government schemes of India are available in one place, with official .gov.in links, without any fees, without any middleman. YojnaSaathi.org is a private website, not a government website, but we take all links to official .gov.in sites.

Today our website has 1357 schemes - 340 Central Government schemes like PM Kisan, Ayushman Bharat, PM Awas, E-Shram, Ration Card, Ujjwala, Sukanya Samriddhi, PM Vishwakarma, Atal Pension, Mudra Loan, and 900+ schemes from 28 states like Delhi Ladli Yojana, UP Kanya Sumangala, Bihar Kanya Utthan, MP Ladli Behna, Rajasthan Berojgari Bhatta, Maharashtra Lek Ladki, Gujarat Vahli Dikri, Haryana Pension, Punjab Shagun Yojana.

Our mission is that every poor, every farmer, every woman, every student should know about their scheme and can apply without any fees. We do not make any documents, do not charge any fees, only give correct information with official .gov.in links. Our team checks new schemes daily and adds them to the website.
