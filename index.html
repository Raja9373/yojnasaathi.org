About Us - YojnaSaathi.org

YojnaSaathi.org ki shuruaat 2024 me hui thi jab mere pados ki ek aunty ko ration card banwane ke liye 1500 rupaye dene pade the. Unko pata hi nahi tha ki ration card nfsa.gov.in pe free me banta hai. Tab mujhe samajh aaya ki desh me 1200+ sarkari yojanaye chal rahi hai lekin 80% logo ko pata hi nahi hai.

Main Alok Mohan Sharma, Delhi se, aur mera bhai Maddy, hum dono ne socha ki ek aisi website banayenge jaha Bharat ki saari sarkari yojanaye ek jagah mile, official .gov.in link ke saath, bina kisi fees ke, bina kisi dalal ke. YojnaSaathi.org private website hai, government website nahi hai, lekin hum saare links official .gov.in pe le jaate hai.

Aaj hamari website pe 1357 yojanaye hai - Central Government ki 340 yojanaye jaise PM Kisan, Ayushman Bharat, PM Awas, E-Shram, Ration Card, Ujjwala, Sukanya Samriddhi, PM Vishwakarma, Atal Pension, Mudra Loan, aur 28 states ki 900+ yojanaye jaise Delhi Ladli Yojana, UP Kanya Sumangala, Bihar Kanya Utthan, MP Ladli Behna, Rajasthan Berojgari Bhatta, Maharashtra Lek Ladki, Gujarat Vahli Dikri, Haryana Pension, Punjab Shagun Yojana.

Hamara mission hai ki har garib, har kisan, har mahila, har student ko apni yojna ka pata chale aur wo bina kisi fees ke apply kar sake.
